BEST BANK IN KENYA AND BANKING AWARDS


Equity Bank commenced business on registration in 1984. It has evolved from a Building Society, a Microfinance Institution to now the all-inclusive Nairobi Stock Exachange and Uganda Securities Exchange public listed Commercial Bank.

With over 4.1 million accounts, accounting over 52% of all bank accounts in Kenya, Equity Bank is the largest bank in the region in terms of customer base.The solidnesss of Equity Bank is underpinned by its shareholder's funds base of over Kshs 19 billion, making Equity Bank one of most capitalized banks in the region.

Equity Bank has received both local and global accolades for its unique and transformation financial model. The bank is credited for taking banking services to the people through its accesible, affortable and flexible service provision.
